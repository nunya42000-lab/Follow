Game Modes

Select your preferred mode in Settings > Game.

  • Simon Says: The pattern grows every round. First "1", then "1, 5", then "1, 5, 9". You must enter the full history.
  • Unique Rounds: Every round is a completely new sequence. The previous numbers are cleared. Ideal for tracking random machine outputs.

Inputs

Choose between 9-Key, 12-Key, or Piano layouts to match your machine.

Practice Mode üéì

Train your memory without a machine. Turn this ON in Settings > Game.

How to Play:
  1. The game will generate a random sequence and play it for you.
  2. Wait for the playback to finish.
  3. Repeat the sequence using the keypad.
  4. If correct, the game speaks "Correct" and starts the next round (longer sequence).
  5. If wrong, the game speaks "Wrong" and you must try again.

Tools & Sensors

  • Blackout Mode: Shake your device firmly. The screen will turn completely black to save battery and stealth your usage. Touch inputs still work! Shake again to wake up.
  • Speed Delete: Tap Backspace to delete one. Hold Backspace to rapidly clear the entire sequence.
  • Microphone Input: Listens for high-pitch beeps or whistles. Go to Settings > General > Calibrate to tune the sensitivity.
  • Camera Input: Detects screen flashes (Red/Blue/Green/Yellow). Point your camera at the game screen.
