The Goal üéØ

This is a sandbox tool designed to help you beat the "Follow" memory portion of skill-based machines.

How to use: Select your input method (9-Key is standard). When the machine flashes a number, press it here. The app tracks the full sequence so you don't have to memorize it.

ATM Icon (üèß): Opens the Redeem barcode for easy cashout at compatible kiosks.

Support ‚òï: If this app helps you win, use the Coffee icon in Settings to send a tip!

Settings Glossary ‚öôÔ∏è

Game Mode
  • Simon Says: For machines where the pattern repeats and adds one new number each time.
  • Unique: For machines that generate a completely new random sequence every round.
  • Auto-Advance: (Unique Only) Automatically clears the screen and moves to the next round after playback finishes.
Playback Controls
  • Autoplay: Automatically plays back the sequence when you enter a new number.
  • Audio Speech: Reads the numbers out loud.
  • Chunk Size: Breaks long sequences into groups (e.g., 3 numbers at a time) for easier memorization.
  • Delay: Adds a pause between machines or chunks.
Stealth & Shortcuts ü•∑
  • Stealth Toggle (Hold '1'): If enabled, holding '1' for 1 second hides the UI.
  • Speed Delete: Hold 'Backspace' to rapidly delete the entire sequence.
  • Blackout Mode: Shake your phone firmly to turn the screen completely black. Shake again to wake.
  • Long Press Play: Hold the 'Play' button to quickly toggle Autoplay on/off without opening settings.
  • Long Press Settings (‚öôÔ∏è): Hold for 1 second to quickly open the Redeem/ATM screen.
  • Stop Playback: Press 'Play' again while a sequence is running to stop it immediately.
